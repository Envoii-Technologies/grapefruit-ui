### Shadow

The amount of box-shadow the Card Component throws, can be changed by passing either `off`, `light`, `medium` or `hard` as a `string` to the `shadow` property.

> **Tipp:** The according box-shadow values are: 
> - off: `0.00`
> - light: `0.05` 
> - medium: `0.10`
> - hard: `0.15`

## Animation

By default, the GuideCard component has an scaling animation on hover, increasing the size of the GuideCard by 1.05. This can be deactivated by setting the `hasAnimation` property to false.

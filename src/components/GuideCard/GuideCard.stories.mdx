## Content

The GuideCard component cansupply informations about the amount of content, that is attached to the components link. This is meant to be passed by the CardList component but can be overwritten.

### Topics

The amount of Topics can be specified by passing a `number` to the `topicAmount` property.

> **Note:** 0 or more than 1 will show as multiple entries, i.E. "Topics".

### Instructions

The amount of Instructions can be specified by passing a `number` to the `instructionAmount` property.

> **Note:** 0 or more than 1 will show as multiple entries, i.E. "Instructions".
